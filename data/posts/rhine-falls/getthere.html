From Zurich HB (main station):
Take an S-Bahn or regional train to Schaffhausen (~1 hour). From Schaffhausen, either:

  • Take a direct train to Neuhausen Rheinfall station (~4 min), or
  • Take bus no. 1 or 6 to Neuhausen Zentrum and walk (~10 min) to the falls.

From Zurich Airport:
Take a direct train to Schaffhausen (~40 min), then follow the above options.

From other cities (e.g. Lucerne, Basel):
Travel to Zurich HB or Winterthur, then change to a train towards Schaffhausen or Neuhausen Rheinfall.
